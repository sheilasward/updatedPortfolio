About Me



Sheila Ward - Nov 2017

I have been an Application Developer for over 20 years in the insurance industry, and I love coding!

I have worked with a variety of languages and platforms during my employment at Lincoln Financial, until my job was eliminated because of outsourcing. Most of my work at Lincoln included Mainframe programming in COBOL, DB2, Easytrieve Plus, SAS, and of course, JCL. However, while there I taught myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and learned enough PHP to write a program for production. I also spent several years learning and writing Crystal Reports, and the ways to join databases to produce those reports. During the last few years I was there, I wrote Javascript to gather information from LifeComm to feed Lincoln's Customer Service Accelerator system.

Since leaving Lincoln, I have been studying Web Development and Android App Development at GTCC, and Full Stack Web Development at UNC Chapel Hill's Coding Bootcamp.

In my spare time, I enjoy visiting with family and serving on the Production Team at my church.
